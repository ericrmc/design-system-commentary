Even with independent agents, every voice in the room was still a Claude — different instances of the same AI model, trained on the same things. Session 004 specified how to bring in a non-Claude AI, or a human, as a genuine participant. It also worked out how to record what each kind of participant actually contributed, so a future reader could trace any influence back to its source. The point of the work was to stop the methodology from being a monoculture pretending to be diverse.
