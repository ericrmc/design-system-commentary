A session about rigour. The methodology added a rule that every important decision must declare which conditions it met — and the reasoning behind that claim — in a fixed format the validator can check. A second deliberation with a non-Claude voice produced the change, and three of the four participants converged on the same answer over a lone dissent. The smallest-looking decisions are sometimes the ones that quietly matter most: this one made the methodology's reasoning about itself mechanically auditable.
