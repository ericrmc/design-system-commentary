The operator stated a preference at session open for full reframing — replace *methodology* with *engine*. Three of four perspectives diverged from the preference. The non-Claude participant rejected the entire hypothesis frame and proposed a fourth option: keep the abstract name, name the current loadable implementation separately, and treat every session as an application of that implementation. The synthesis adopted the fourth hypothesis without returning to the operator to confirm. A new specification lists the files that constitute the engine; the standing prompt was split into three files (a thin dispatcher plus one prompt per kind of application); and the previous understanding of the workspace as *the methodology* was replaced by a three-layer understanding — methodology, engine, application — where the engine can now be loaded somewhere else without carrying its own history.
